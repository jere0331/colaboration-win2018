THE STUDIO

Lola Melani founded her private studio in New York in 2016 in the heart of the vibrant and trendy Chelsea neighborhood. The warm and inviting space offers personalized attention and comfort tailored to each individual client and features large windows that offer soft, natural light perfect for photography. Luxurious tufted lounge furniture, a seductive wall of roses, soothing candles, exclusive hand painted backdrops and elegant music create an immediately tranquil atmosphere. With over a decade of experience, Lola brings a truly intimate approach to her services with ease while creating an unforgettable final product to be appreciated for years to come.
